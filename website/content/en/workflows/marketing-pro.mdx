# Marketing Pro Workflow

A comprehensive collection of 38 skills for marketing professionals, campaign managers, and growth teams.

## Target Users

- Digital marketers
- Growth hackers
- Product marketing managers (PMMs)
- Campaign strategists
- Brand managers

## Installation

```bash
npx add-skill nicepkg/ai-workflow/workflows/marketing-pro-workflow
```

## Key Capabilities

- **Strategy** - Go-to-market planning, buyer personas, pricing strategy
- **Campaign Management** - UTM tracking, email sequences, landing pages
- **Analytics** - ROI analysis, funnel analysis, executive dashboards
- **Content** - Ad copy, social media, SEO optimization
- **Lead Generation** - Cold outreach, personalization at scale
